Benefits of Regular Checkups

  1. Prevention: Catch issues early before they escalate.
  2. Health Links: Oral health impacts heart and metabolic health.
  3. Confidence: Clean teeth boost self-esteem and comfort.

Tips for Better Oral Health

  • Brush twice daily with fluoride toothpaste.
  • Floss regularly to remove plaque between teeth.
  • Limit sugary snacks and drinks.
